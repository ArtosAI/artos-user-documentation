Welcome to the Artos Documentation Hub. This guide is designed to help you quickly learn how to use the Artos platform to draft, review, manage, and collaborate on documents in your day-to-day workflow.

Artos is purpose-built for biotech and pharmaceutical organizations. Whether you're drafting CSRs, Module 2 summaries, protocols, IND/IMPD components, or internal reports, Artos helps your teams work faster and more effectively.

## Our Philosophy

At Artos, we like to think of AI as a multiplier of your productivity and skill. It's certainly not meant to be a replacement.

As such, you're still going to need to bring all the skills you have to working with the Artos platform. As with any tool, there is a learning curve, but we're here to help.
