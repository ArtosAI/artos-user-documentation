## Dedicated Support Plans

For teams on dedicated support plans:

- **Dedicated Contact** — A specific Artos team member assigned to your account, with deep familiarity of your templates and workflows, plus faster response times
- **Regularly Scheduled Office Hours** — Recurring sessions for ongoing needs, proactive template optimization, and strategic planning for new document types

Interested in a dedicated support plan? Reach out to discuss how we can best support your team.

## What to Include When Contacting Support

**For Template Questions:**

- Template name and document type
- Specific section or action you're asking about
- What you're trying to achieve
- Example of expected vs. actual behavior (if applicable)

**For Document Generation Issues:**

- Document name and template used
- Description of the issue
- Screenshots or examples of unexpected output (if applicable)
- Any context files used

**For Technical Issues:**

- Error messages or codes (if any)
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Browser or system information (if relevant)
- File types and sizes involved

**For General Questions:**

- Context about your workflow or use case
- What you've already tried
- Timeline or urgency level
